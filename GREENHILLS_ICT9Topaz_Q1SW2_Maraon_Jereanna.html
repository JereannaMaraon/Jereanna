Characters in Noli Me Tangere









Maria Clara A very beautiful young lady, soon to be wife of Crisostomo Ibarra
Padre Damaso A very cruel and mean priest. He is also the real father of Maria Clara.
Crisostomo Ibarra Son of Don Rafael who studied in Europe for 7 years. The future husband of Maria Clara.
Kapitan Tiago A very rich widowed man who raised Maria Clara. He's married to Pia Alba.
Pia Alba Wife of Kapitan Tiago, passed away shortly after giving birth to Maria Clara.
